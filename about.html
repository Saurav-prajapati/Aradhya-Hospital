About Us

New Aradhya Hospital, located in Bapu Ji Complex, Aligarh, is a trusted name in advanced and affordable healthcare. With a mission to provide compassionate, patient-centered treatment, we offer a wide range of medical services — from emergency care to specialized surgeries — all under one roof.

Led by experienced doctors and supported by skilled medical staff, our hospital is equipped with modern facilities like ICU, ventilators, trauma care, neuro surgery, orthopedics, pathology lab, digital X-ray, physiotherapy, and more. We also actively support government healthcare initiatives like Ayushman Bharat and Pandit Deendayal Yojana, ensuring quality healthcare is accessible to all.

At New Aradhya Hospital, we believe in healing with empathy, treating every patient with dignity, and delivering medical care that meets global standards. Your health and safety are our priority — today, tomorrow, and always.

Why Choose Us

At New Aradhya Hospital, we go beyond treatment — we deliver trust, care, and excellence. Our commitment to high-quality healthcare and patient satisfaction makes us a preferred choice for families across Aligarh and beyond.

Comprehensive Medical Services

From emergency care to specialized surgeries, diagnostics, and maternity services — we provide a complete range of healthcare under one roof.

Expert Doctors & Staff

Our team of qualified doctors, surgeons, and healthcare professionals brings experience, dedication, and personalized care to every patient.

Modern Facilities & Equipment

We are equipped with advanced medical technology including ICU, ventilators, digital X-ray, pathology labs, and well-maintained operation theaters.

Affordable & Accessible Care
